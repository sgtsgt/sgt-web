Overview

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Telecom Toolkit mobile application ("the App"), published by Samskill Global Telecom ("we", "our", or "us"), accessible at samskillglobaltelecom.guru.

The Telecom Toolkit is a free, offline utility application designed to help individuals, businesses, and telecom professionals with everyday connectivity tasks such as estimating data usage, converting units, calculating download times, and troubleshooting connections.

The short version: The Telecom Toolkit does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data about you at any point or in any way. There is nothing to disclose beyond that fact. The rest of this policy explains how and why.

By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Data collection

2. What we collect

We collect nothing. The Telecom Toolkit does not collect any information about you, your device, your location, your usage habits, or any inputs you enter into the App.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information
  • Your device's IMEI, advertising ID, or hardware identifiers
  • Your IP address or network information
  • Your geographic location or GPS coordinates
  • The values, inputs, or results from any tools you use within the App
  • Crash reports, analytics events, or usage statistics
  • App session durations, screens visited, or buttons tapped
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or browser fingerprints

There is no user account, no database of user records, and no server that receives information from your copy of the App.

Technical

3. How the App works

The Telecom Toolkit is a fully offline application. Once installed on your device, it requires no internet connection to function. Here is a plain-language description of how it operates:

  1. All computation is local. Every calculation, lookup, or decision-tree in the App runs entirely on your device's processor. No data is sent to any server to produce a result.
  2. All content is pre-loaded. The glossary, troubleshooting guides, reference tables, and tool logic are bundled inside the App at install time. There is no content fetched from the internet.
  3. No outbound connections. The App makes no network requests at any time — not to Samskill's servers, not to any analytics platform, not to any third-party service.
  4. No background activity. The App does not run in the background, does not schedule jobs, and does not phone home when your device is idle.

Exception — initial install: Like any app, downloading the Telecom Toolkit from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store involves that store's own network and privacy practices. Samskill has no visibility into or control over those interactions. Please consult Google's or Apple's privacy policies for details on what they may record during a download.

Local storage

6. Local storage on your device

Some features of the Telecom Toolkit may save data locally on your device to improve your experience. For example, saving inputs you last entered into a calculator so you don't have to re-enter them. This is called "local storage" or "on-device storage."

  • All locally stored data remains on your device. It is never transmitted to Samskill or any other party.
  • You are in full control. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's data or cache in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.
  • What may be stored locally: calculator inputs, selected tool preferences, and theme settings (e.g. dark/light mode). Nothing that identifies you personally.
  • What is never stored locally: your name, contact information, or any information that could be linked to you as an individual.

Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.
